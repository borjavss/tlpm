Welcome to "The LAST Predictions Machine"

This is a funny project which aims to get the most flexible prediction software in the world. The purpose is to get a R software library to quickly solve hard prediction problems with amazing accuracy.

Aimed Solutions

Get a less 5 min forecasting with incredible accuracy from any given subject data incoming. We want to start giving solutions to subjects related with environmental concerns (like producing weather forecasting to renewable energy producers) and, we will follow by giving solutions to social problems by future predictions.

TLPM Machine Processes

The following phases will be deployed inside our "TLPM Machine" to get our prediction:

  • An easy tool package helps to quickly get our "tidy data" from any given "raw data".
  • Select your "forecasting mode" (it could be as simple as a "linear regression" or heavy predictions methods like "Artificial Neural Networks").
  • "Extra options" like a "mixture of all methods" (by using boosting supervised learning method) or adding other adaptative methods to predictions.
